# Investment Framework

## Overview
This comprehensive framework outlines all collateral, analysis, and documentation required for evaluating and presenting investment opportunities. It combines core investment analysis requirements with investor-specific due diligence needs.

## Part 1: Core Investment Analysis Collateral

### **1. Strategic Foundation**
- [ ] **Vision Brief Summary**
  - Executive summary
  - Problem statement
  - Solution overview
  - Value proposition
- [ ] **Market Analysis**
  - TAM/SAM/SOM with validation
  - Market growth projections
  - Market segmentation
  - Market timing analysis
- [ ] **Competitive Analysis & Positioning**
  - Competitive landscape mapping
  - Competitive positioning strategy
  - Differentiation analysis
  - Competitive advantages and moats
  - Barriers to entry
- [ ] **Risk Analysis**
  - Technical risks
  - Market risks
  - Financial risks
  - Operational risks

### **2. Business Requirements & Planning**

## Key Investment Criteria

### **Must-Have Criteria**
1. **Market Opportunity**: Minimum $100M TAM
2. **Competitive Advantage**: Unique technology or sustainable moat
3. **Team Capability**: Right expertise, experience, and network
4. **Financial Viability**: Clear path to profitability
5. **Strategic Fit**: Aligns with investor thesis

### **Valuation Methodology**
- **Market Approach**: Comparable company analysis
- **Income Approach**: DCF based on projected cash flows
- **Asset Approach**: Technology and IP valuation
- **Risk Adjustment**: Market, technology, execution risks

### **Return Expectations**
- **Early Stage**: 10-20x return potential
- **Growth Stage**: 5-10x return potential
- **Late Stage**: 2-5x return potential
- **IRR Target**: 25-40% depending on stage

## Template Usage Guidelines

This framework should be applied to each investment opportunity with:
- **Customization**: Adapt for industry-specific requirements
- **Stage-Appropriate**: Scale detail based on company stage
- **Regular Updates**: Refresh based on market feedback
- **Integration**: Connect with existing business processes
- **Evidence-Based**: Support all claims with concrete data
