1. Ninja Notes - Go-to-Market Plan

Target Market & Positioning

Primary ICP (Ideal Customer Profile):

  1. Mid-Market Enterprises (500-5,000 employees) - 70% of focus
    • Sales teams, product teams, executive leadership
    • 10-50+ hours/week of meetings
    • Pain: Meeting overload, action item tracking, knowledge loss
  2. Enterprise (5,000+ employees) - 25% of focus
    • Department-level deployments (50-500 seats)
    • Compliance requirements (SOC 2, ISO, GDPR)
    • Pain: Governance, audit trails, integration with enterprise systems
  3. SMB/Power Users (1-500 employees) - 5% of focus
    • Consultants, freelancers, small teams
    • Product-led growth (PLG) funnel
    • Pain: Capturing client conversations, billable time tracking
Positioning Statement:
"Ninja Notes is the enterprise-grade AI meeting intelligence platform that transforms conversations into actionable insights, compliance-ready documentation, and seamless CRM integration - trusted by companies who require SOC 2, ISO 27001, and GDPR compliance."

Distribution Channels

3-Channel Strategy (diversified to reduce risk):

Channel % of Users (2026 → 2028) Revenue Retention CAC Characteristics
Direct (PLG) 60% → 30% 100% $50 Self-serve, freemium funnel, digital marketing
Resellers/VARs 30% → 40% 70% $30 System integrators, MSPs, consultancies
HP Partnership 10% → 30% 60% $15 Bundled with HP laptops, enterprise accounts

Channel Evolution:

  • Year 1 (2026): Direct-led (60%) - prove product-market fit
  • Year 2 (2027): Reseller growth (40%) - scale through partners
  • Year 3 (2028): HP dominance (30%) - channel leverage, low CAC

2. Omni Sales & Service - Go-to-Market Plan

Target Market & Positioning

Omni Sales ICP:

  1. Pharmaceutical Sales (30% of revenue)
    • 50,000+ pharma reps in US, 25-100 reps per company
    • Pain: Complex product portfolios, regulatory compliance (FDA, HIPAA)
  2. Construction Equipment Sales (25% of revenue)
    • Heavy machinery dealers, building materials distributors
    • Pain: Technical specs, pricing complexity, field-based selling
  3. General B2B Field Sales (25% of revenue)
    • Industrial, wholesale, distribution companies
    • Pain: Mobile workforce, CRM integration, real-time pricing

Omni Service ICP:

  1. HVAC, Plumbing, Electrical (40% of revenue)
    • 1M+ field technicians in US, 20-500 technicians per company
    • Pain: Predictive maintenance, diagnostics, parts inventory
  2. Facilities Management (30% of revenue)
    • Corporate campuses, hospitals, universities
    • Pain: Work order management, compliance tracking, multi-site
  3. Manufacturing Service (20% of revenue)
    • Equipment maintenance, quality inspections
    • Pain: Downtime cost ($50K-$500K/hour), expert knowledge loss
Positioning Statement:
"Omni is the AI-first field operations platform that empowers mobile sales teams and service technicians with real-time intelligence, predictive insights, and seamless workflow automation - 60% less cost and 80% faster implementation than ServiceNow or Salesforce."

Distribution Channels

2-Channel Strategy:

Channel % of Customers Characteristics
Direct Enterprise Sales 70% Field sales team, 90-120 day sales cycle, $50K+ ACV
Channel Partners (SIs) 30% System integrators, industry consultants, referral partnerships

Note: No HP partnership (different buyer, field operations not laptop-bundled)
