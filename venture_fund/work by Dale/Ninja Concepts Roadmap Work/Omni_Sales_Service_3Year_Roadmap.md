## Executive Summary

**Omni Sales** and **Omni Service** are enterprise AI platforms designed for field operations - empowering mobile sales teams and field service technicians with real-time AI guidance, predictive intelligence, and seamless workflow automation.

**Why Combined Roadmap**: These products share 80% of their infrastructure (mobile apps, voice AI, field worker UX, CRM integration) and target the same enterprise buyer (VP Operations, COO). Building them together accelerates time-to-market and reduces development costs by 60%.

**3-Year Vision**: Become the leading AI platform for field operations with 10,794 users across 257 enterprise customers, $13.16M ARR, and market leadership in construction, field service, and pharmaceutical sales.

3. **4-8 Week Implementation** (vs. 6-12 months for enterprise FSM)
4. **Mobile-First UX** (built for field workers, not office workers)
5. **Voice-AI Integration** (hands-free for technicians, leverages Ninja Notes engine)
