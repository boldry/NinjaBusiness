## Executive Summary

## Product Strategy

### Target Market

**Primary Segments:**
1. **Mid-Market Enterprises** (500-5,000 employees) - 70% of revenue
   - Sales teams, product teams, executive leadership
   - Distributed/hybrid workforce
   - High meeting volume (10-50 hours/week)

2. **Enterprise** (5,000+ employees) - 25% of revenue
   - Department-level deployments
   - Compliance requirements (SOC 2, ISO)
   - Integration with enterprise systems (Salesforce, ServiceNow)

3. **SMB/Individuals** (1-500 employees) - 5% of revenue
   - Power users, freelancers, consultants
   - Product-led growth (freemium)
   - Viral loop into enterprise

### Competitive Positioning

**Direct Competitors:**
- Otter.ai ($100M ARR, consumer-focused, limited enterprise)
- Fireflies.ai ($1B valuation, $10.9M revenue, momentum but smaller)
- Notion AI ($500M ARR, AI add-on to workspace tool)
- Microsoft Teams (bundled transcription, "good enough" but shallow)

**Our Differentiation:**
1. **Enterprise-First**: SOC 2, ISO 27001, GDPR, EU AI Act compliant
2. **Ecosystem Play**: Integrates with Omni, Sona (multi-product intelligence)
3. **Channel Strategy**: HP partnership + reseller network (70% of users, low CAC)
4. **Action Intelligence**: Not just transcription - tasks, follow-ups, CRM sync
5. **Vertical Customization**: Healthcare, construction, financial services models
