## Risk Factors & Mitigation

### Revenue Risks

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|--------|------------|
| **HP partnership underperforms (10% vs. 30% of users)** | Medium (40%) | High | Diversify channels (resellers, direct PLG), Dell/Lenovo partnerships |
| **Enterprise sales cycles lengthen (>6 months)** | Medium (45%) | Medium | Pilot programs (30-60 days), land-and-expand, SOC 2 fast-track |

| **Services cannibalize product sales (customers prefer services)** | Low (20%) | Low | Incentivize product attach (bundle discounts), product-first positioning |
