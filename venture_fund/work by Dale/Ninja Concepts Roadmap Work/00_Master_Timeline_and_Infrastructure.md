## 4. Compliance & Certification Timeline

Enterprise customers require security and compliance certifications. This is **non-negotiable** for sales.

### Certification Roadmap

#### **SOC 2 Type I (Target: Q2 2026)**

**Timeline:**
- **Jan-Feb 2026**: Readiness assessment, gap analysis
- **Mar-Apr 2026**: Remediation (policies, controls, security hardening)
- **May-Jun 2026**: Audit execution (vCISO + external auditor)
- **Jun 30, 2026**: Certification issued

**Requirements:**
- Information security policies (ISMS)
- Access controls (RBAC, MFA, SSO)
- Data encryption (at-rest, in-transit)
- Incident response plan
- Vendor management
- Change management
- Business continuity/disaster recovery

**Cost:** $50K (audit + vCISO + Vanta subscription)

**Impact:**
- **Unlocks**: 60%+ of enterprise buyers require SOC 2
- **Revenue**: $500K-$1M incremental ARR (enterprise deals)
- **Competitive**: Differentiator vs. smaller competitors

---

#### **ISO 27001 (Target: Q4 2027)**

**Timeline:**
- **Q4 2026**: Kick-off, ISMS documentation
- **Q1-Q2 2027**: Implementation, risk assessment, controls
- **Q3 2027**: Internal audit, management review
- **Q4 2027**: Certification audit, certificate issued

**Requirements (build on SOC 2):**
- Risk management framework
- Asset management
- Supplier security
- Physical security (data centers)
- Human resources security
- Cryptographic controls

**Cost:** $75K (certification body + consulting)

**Impact:**
- **Unlocks**: International expansion (EMEA requires ISO)
- **Revenue**: $2M-$5M incremental ARR (European customers)
- **Competitive**: Enterprise-grade credibility

---

#### **GDPR Compliance (Ongoing, Critical by Q2 2026)**

**Requirements:**
- Data Processing Agreements (DPAs) with customers
- Right to erasure, data portability
- Consent management
- Data breach notification (<72 hours)
- Privacy by design

**Cost:** $20K (legal + privacy tools)

**Impact:**
- **Required**: Cannot sell in EU without GDPR compliance
- **Risk**: Fines up to €20M or 4% of revenue

---

#### **EU AI Act Compliance (Target: Q2 2028)**

**Timeline:**
- **Q3 2027**: Impact assessment, classification (general purpose AI)
- **Q4 2027**: Documentation, transparency requirements
- **Q1 2028**: Risk management, testing
- **Q2 2028**: Compliance declaration

**Requirements:**
- AI model documentation (training data, capabilities, limitations)
- Risk assessment for AI use cases
- Transparency (users must know they're interacting with AI)
- Human oversight mechanisms
- Copyright compliance (training data)

**Cost:** $60K (legal + compliance consulting)

**Impact:**
- **Required**: EU market access (August 2, 2025 deadline for GPAI)
- **Competitive**: Few AI startups compliant by 2028

   - Prepay incentives (25% discount for annual vs. monthly)

   - Defer 3 hires from Q1 to Q3 2026 (save $120K)
   - Reduce marketing spend by 30% (save $50K)
   - Negotiate vendor payment terms (Net 60 vs. Net 30)

   - **Terms**: Convertible note, $15M cap (50% premium to Seed), 8% interest
