## Strategy

### SME Vetting Process
**Rigorous Assessment Framework:**
1. **Domain Expertise Validation** - Deep industry knowledge and market access
2. **Market Opportunity Analysis** - Clear TAM, competitive landscape, and growth potential
3. **Technical Feasibility** - AI solution viability and Ninja Concepts execution capability
4. **Business Model Validation** - Revenue streams, unit economics, and scalability
5. **Long-term Viability** - Market sustainability and competitive moats
6. **Execution Readiness** - Team capability, resources, and timeline alignment

**Vetting Criteria:**
- **SME Credentials**: Proven track record in target industry/domain
- **Market Validation**: Existing customer relationships or pilot opportunities
- **AI Integration**: Clear use case for AI/ML technology enhancement
- **Scalability**: Business model supports 10x+ growth potential
- **Execution Fit**: Alignment with Ninja Concepts' technical capabilities

### Investment Identification and Position
**Target SME Profile:**
- **Industry Experts**: Deep domain knowledge in healthcare, finance, construction, etc.
- **Market Access**: Existing customer relationships or distribution channels
- **AI Vision**: Clear understanding of how AI can transform their industry
- **Execution Partnership**: Willing to work collaboratively with Ninja Concepts
- **Growth Ambition**: Commitment to building scalable, high-value businesses

**Investment Positioning:**
- **Capital + Execution**: Fund provides capital, Ninja Concepts provides technical execution
- **Shared Success**: Equity participation aligns all parties for long-term value creation
- **Risk Mitigation**: Proven execution partner reduces technical and operational risk
- **Portfolio Synergy**: Cross-venture learning and shared infrastructure benefits
