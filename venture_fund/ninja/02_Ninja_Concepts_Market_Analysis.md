1. **Enterprise Trust**: Requires proven team (we have), references, case studies

**Economic Risks:**
- **Budget Constraints**: If recession/downturn, IT budgets may contract
  - *Mitigation*: Strong ROI focus, essential productivity tools, services can flex with demand
- **Competition Intensity**: Big Tech (Microsoft, Google) or Big 4 (Deloitte, Accenture) entering markets
  - *Mitigation*: Speed, specialization, mid-market focus (underserved by large players)

**Regulatory Risks:**
- **AI Regulations**: Overly restrictive AI regulations could slow adoption
  - *Mitigation*: Compliance expertise = competitive advantage, not liability
  - *Opportunity*: EU AI Act creates demand for our compliance services
- **Data Privacy**: Increasing privacy restrictions could limit AI training data
  - *Mitigation*: Privacy-first architecture, on-premise/hybrid deployment options

**Operational Risks:**
- **Talent Acquisition**: Competition for AI/ML engineers intense
  - *Mitigation*: Remote-first, competitive compensation, equity, meaningful work

1. Ease of use (field workers are non-technical)

4. Integration with existing systems (CRM, ERP, scheduling)
